# Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

> An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze supermarket sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, branch performance, and product trends through data-driven visualizations and business insights.

![Dashboard Preview](Images/DashboardOverview.png)

# 📖 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, raw data alone does not provide actionable insights for business decision-making.

This project demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used as a complete Business Intelligence tool to transform raw supermarket sales data into an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, understand customer behavior, and support strategic business decisions.

The project follows the complete analytics lifecycle, including data cleaning, transformation, KPI development, pivot table analysis, visualization, and business insight generation.

# Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze supermarket sales data and answer key business questions such as:

- Which branch generates the highest revenue?
- Which product line contributes the most to total sales?
- Which payment method is most preferred by customers?
- Which customer segment spends the most?
- During which hours does the supermarket experience peak sales?
- How do customer ratings vary across branches?
- What business opportunities can be identified from the available data?

# Key Business Insights

Based on the analysis, the following insights were identified:

- **Giza** emerged as the highest-performing branch based on sales.
- **Food & Beverages** generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- **Member** customers recorded a higher average basket value than normal customers.
- **E-wallet** was the most preferred payment method.
- **Evening** represented the peak shopping period.
- Customer ratings indicate a consistently positive shopping experience across all branches.

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analytical findings, the following recommendations are suggested:

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories to avoid stock shortages.
- Strengthen customer loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.
- Schedule additional staff during evening hours to improve customer service.
- Continue promoting digital payment methods for faster and more convenient transactions.
- Investigate the performance of lower-performing branches to identify growth opportunities.
